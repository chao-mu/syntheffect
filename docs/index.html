What is SynthEffect?

SynthEffect is an analog-inspired software video synthesizer for creative coders and VJs who would rather learn an API than a UI. Build captivating visual art to your exact specifications, controlled by your favorite video game joysticks and midi controllers, for free.

Currently SynthEffect is currently pre-alpha. If you would like to learn when the alpha officially launches, for our announce list

Text over GUI

Build a rack of video modules with your favorite text editor. Want more control? Patch in your favorite video game controllers, sound files, midi controllers, or as many combinations thereof as you like!

Analog Inspired

Patch together modules to build expressive effects and interactions, bringing in input from media files and hardware or generating from within

Open Source

We believe strongly both in empowering our community and in the merrits of free and open source software. SynthEffect is and will always be open source.
